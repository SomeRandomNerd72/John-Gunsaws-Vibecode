Disable Infighting/Rebellion
If an enemy's bullet passes through another enemy, there is a 5% chance that they are reassigned to the “rebel” team and become hostile to every other team. Team goodguys bypasses the calculation.
The problem is that rebels are not exempted from the calculation, and as such may begin shooting each other without dealing damage, or kicking each other, which will.
By adding the rebels to the exemptions, they will never infight. This can be useful if you want enemies who never infight, though if you have dnSpy you should be able to add your own teams, just note that it will only affect your copy of the game unless you share your dll aswell.

Enemy Weapon Drop Modifications
There are 3 new configurations for enemy weapon drops, the options being: no drops, drops without ammo, and drops reserve ammo. Simply input your desired drop type into the enemy's crate damage field in the editor. Drop type is changed by the 10s digit and crate damage is changed by the 1s digit. 
Values are: 0 for regular drops, 1 for no drops, 2 for drops without ammo, and 3 for drops reserve ammo. Crate damage is the same as before where 0 is damage disabled and 1 is damage enabled. So if I wanted regular drops with crate damage enabled, I would leave the field input at the default, since 01 is simply shortened to 1.
